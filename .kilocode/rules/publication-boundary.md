# Public-repository boundary

Follow `AGENTS.md` and
`qor/references/doctrine-publication-boundary.md`.

Delete or anonymize direct references to repositories outside Qor-logic.
Outside repositories may inform private reasoning only. Do not create imports,
dependencies, destinations, paths, credentials, or operational coupling that
identify or hardcode a specific outside repository. Generic
operator-configured interfaces are permitted. The sole exception is a
deliberate, public-safe record under `docs/Lessons-Learned/`.
